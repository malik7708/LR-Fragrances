Shipping Policy

Carefully crafted delivery standards for a premium fragrance experience

Delivery Coverage

LR Fragrances proudly delivers across Pakistan. We partner with trusted national courier services to ensure safe, secure, and timely delivery of every order.

Order Processing Time

All orders are processed within 24–48 hours after confirmation. Orders placed on weekends or public holidays are processed on the next working day.

Estimated Delivery Timeline

  • Major Cities: 2–4 business days
  • Other Regions: 3–5 business days

Delivery timelines may vary due to weather conditions, courier delays, or unforeseen logistical factors.

Shipping Charges

Shipping charges are calculated at checkout based on your delivery location. Promotional campaigns may include free shipping for eligible orders.

Order Tracking

Once your order is dispatched, tracking details will be shared via email or SMS. You may also track your shipment using the Track Order option on our website.

Delivery Assurance

Every LR Fragrances order is carefully packaged to preserve product quality. In case of delivery issues, our support team is always ready to assist you.
