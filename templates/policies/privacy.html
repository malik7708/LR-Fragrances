Privacy Policy

Your privacy is treated with the same care and refinement as our fragrances

Commitment to Your Privacy

At LR Fragrances, protecting your personal information is a fundamental part of our relationship with you. We are committed to maintaining the confidentiality, integrity, and security of all data shared with us, ensuring that your trust in our brand is always respected.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your information when you interact with our website, place an order, or communicate with our team. By using our services, you acknowledge and agree to the practices outlined in this policy.

When you place an order or register with LR Fragrances, we may collect personal information such as your name, contact details, shipping address, and order history. This information allows us to process transactions efficiently and deliver your purchases accurately.

Additionally, we may collect non-personal information including browser type, device information, IP address, and website usage patterns. This data helps us enhance website performance, improve user experience, and maintain a secure online environment.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to provide you with a seamless and personalized shopping experience. This includes order fulfillment, delivery updates, customer support, and essential communication related to your purchases.

From time to time, we may use your contact details to share updates about new products, exclusive offers, or important service-related announcements. You may opt out of promotional communications at any time without affecting your ability to shop with us.

Data Protection & Security

LR Fragrances implements strict security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. We utilize secure technologies and trusted service providers to ensure that your data remains protected at all times.

While no online system can guarantee absolute security, we continuously review and enhance our practices to meet industry standards and safeguard your privacy.

LR Fragrances values your trust and remains dedicated to protecting your privacy with elegance and integrity.
